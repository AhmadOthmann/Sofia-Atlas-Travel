# Demo Script

## Three-minute product walkthrough

1. Open the dashboard and introduce Atlas as an operator console for an autonomous travel sales team.
2. Show the pipeline and explain how agents qualify and advance leads.
3. Open a lead to show structured customer context, score, stage, and next action.
4. Open the call view to show transcript visibility and real-time coaching prompts.
5. Show drafts and explain the human approval or takeover path.
6. Show analytics to connect agent activity to qualified leads and conversion outcomes.
7. In a live HappyRobot demo, trigger a conversation and show the dashboard state update.
8. Finish with a completed meeting booking and its trace.

## Honest demo disclosure

When no HappyRobot credentials are configured, state clearly that the dashboard is in demo mode. Do not present fixture data as a live platform integration.
